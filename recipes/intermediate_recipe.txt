Title: Roasted Pork Ribs with Vegetables and Creamy Rice

Cook Time: 75 minutes

Difficulty: Intermediate

Servings: 1

Kitchen Utensils:
- Oven
- Oven tray
- Frying pan
- Pot
- Cutting board
- Knife
- Spoon

Ingredients:
- 10 OZ of pork ribs 
- 4 OZ of potatoes 
- 2 OZ of carrots
- 2 OZ of bell pepper
- 4 OZ of rice
- 2 OZ of sour cream

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.

2. On a cutting board, cut the potatoes and carrots into cubes, and slice the bell pepper.

3. Place the pork ribs on an oven tray, add the potatoes, carrots, and bell pepper. Put the tray in the oven and roast for about an hour or until the pork ribs are cooked through.

4. While the pork ribs are in the oven, wash the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Then cook the rice in a pot according to the package instructions.

5. When the rice is done cooking, remove it from heat and stir in the sour cream until well combined.

6. Once the pork ribs and vegetables are done roasting, remove from the oven and let them rest for a couple of minutes.

7. Serve the roasted pork ribs and vegetables with the creamy rice on the side.

Enjoy your hearty meal.
