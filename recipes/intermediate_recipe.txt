Title: Apple Banana Carrot Delight

Cook Time: 50 minutes 

Difficulty: Medium

Servings: 1

Kitchen Utensils: 
- Cutting board 
- Knife 
- Blender
- Saucepan
- Stirring spoon

Ingredients: 
- 10 OZ of apples
- 6 OZ of bananas
- 4 OZ of carrots 

Instructions:

1. Start by washing and peeling the 10 OZ of apples and 4 OZ of carrots. 

2. Using a cutting board and a knife, slice the apples and carrots into small pieces to make it easier for blending. 

3. Next, peel the 6 OZ of bananas and slice them as well into simpler pieces.

4. Put the sliced fruits and vegetable into the blender and blend until you achieve a smooth puree. 

5. Pour the puree into a saucepan and place over medium heat for about 40 minutes. Stir the mix frequently to keep it from sticking to the base of the pan. 

6. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 10 more minutes or until it thickens to your desired consistency. 

7. Your Apple Banana Carrot Delight is now ready. Pour it into your serving bowl and let it cool before enjoying. This is a nutritious dish packed with essential vitamins and minerals, perfect for breakfast or a mid-day snack. 

Enjoy your homemade fruity and vegetable delight!
