Title: Creamy Chicken and Vegetable Casserole

Cook Time: 75 minutes 

Difficulty: Intermediate 

Kitchen Utensils: 
- Large frying pan 
- Sharp knife 
- Measuring spoons 
- Casserole dish 
- Cutting board 

Ingredients:
- 20 OZ of Chicken breast 
- 14 OZ of Potatoes 
- 13 OZ of Tomato 
- 8 OZ of Bell pepper 
- 12 OZ of Sour cream 
- 4 OZ of Butter 

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).

2. Rinse the chicken breasts, and pat them dry with a paper towel. Then, cut the chicken into smaller pieces around 1 inch in size.

3. Next, chop the potatoes, tomatoes, and bell peppers into similar sizes as the chicken pieces.

4. Heat the large frying pan on a medium-high heat, then add 2 oz of butter to melt.

5. Once the butter is melted, add the chicken pieces into the pan. Cook them until they are browned nicely.

6. After the chicken is browned, remove it from the pan and set it aside. 

7. Add the remaining 2 oz of butter to the pan, then add the chopped potatoes. Cook them until they are golden and crispy, then remove them from the pan and set them aside.

8. Now, add the chopped tomatoes and bell pepper to the pan. Cook them until they are slightly softened.

9. Add the cooked chicken and potatoes back to the pan, then mix everything well. 

10. Gradually stir in the sour cream into the pan, making sure everything is well coated.

11. Transfer the mixture to the casserole dish, spreading it evenly.

12. Place the casserole dish in the preheated oven, and let it bake for around 45 minutes, or until the top becomes golden and the vegetables are fully cooked.

13. Once cooked, remove from the oven and let it cool for few minutes.

14. Serve your delicious creamy chicken and vegetable casserole while it's warm. Enjoy!
