Title: ABC (Apple Banana Carrot) Delight

Cook Time: 60 Minutes 

Difficulty: Intermediate

Servings: 1

Kitchen Utensils: 
- Knife
- Grater
- Saucepan
- Blender
- Spoon

Ingredients:
- 4 OZ of Apple
- 4 OZ of Banana
- 4 OZ of Carrot

Instructions:

1. Start by thoroughly washing your apple, banana, and carrot in clean water.

2. Using your knife, peel the skin off the apple and carrot, then chop them into small pieces. 

3. Peel the skin off the banana too and slice it into small discs. 

4. With your grater, grate the apple and carrot pieces for a smoother texture.

5. In the saucepan, add the grated apple and carrot then cook on medium heat for about 15-20 minutes. 

6. Once the apple and carrot are soft and tender, remove the saucepan from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes.

7. Take your blender, place the cooked apple and carrot mixture along with the banana slices in it.

8. Blend the mixture until it forms a smooth and creamy texture. 

9. Pour the blend into a bowl. You can either eat it immediately or place it in the fridge to cool.

10. Serve your ABC (Apple Banana Carrot) Delight in a serving dish and enjoy!

Note: This recipe is quite flexible. You can add more of any ingredient to suit your taste. Also, you can add spices or sweeteners if you prefer. Just make sure not to stray too far from the original ABC trio.
