Title: Apple Banana Carrot Delight

Cook Time: 50 Minutes

Difficulty: Intermediate

Servings: 1

Kitchen Utensils:
- Knife
- Grater
- Mixing Bowl
- Oven
- Baking Tray

Ingredients:
- 4 OZ of Apple
- 3 OZ of Banana
- 2 OZ of Carrot

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. Start by peeling your apple, banana, and carrot. Then use a knife to slice the apple and banana into thin pieces, but grate the carrot.

3. In a mixing bowl, combine the apple, banana, and carrot. Mix them well to blend the flavors.

4. Grease the baking tray and spread the mixture evenly across the tray. Place it in the preheated oven.

5. Let the mixture bake for about 45 minutes. Check every so often to avoid burning your dish.

6. After 45 minutes, remove the tray from the oven. Be careful, as it will be hot.

7. Allow the final dish to cool down for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your homemade Apple Banana Carrot Delight! Please remember that this dish is best served fresh.
