Title: Apple Banana Carrot Puree

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Servings: 1

Kitchen Utensils:
- knife
- cutting board
- steamer
- blender

Ingredients:
- 3 OZ of apple
- 3 OZ of banana
- 3 OZ of carrot

Instructions:

1. Start by peeling all the fruits and vegetables. Cut the carrot, apple and banana into small pieces.

2. Put the carrot pieces into a steamer over boiling water and cook for 20 minutes.

3. Add the apple pieces to the steamer and cook for an additional 10 minutes.

4. Finally, add the banana pieces to the steamer and cook for another 5-10 minutes or until all the ingredients are soft.

5. Once all the ingredients are adequately softened, remove them from the heat and let them cool.

6. Once cooled, put everything into your blender. Blend until the mixture turns into a smooth puree.

7. Serve in a bowl, enjoy this sweet and healthy Apple Banana Carrot Puree! You can refrigerate the leftovers and they will keep for 2-3 days.

Note: Always consult your doctor before introducing new foods into your baby's diet, especially if your baby has food allergies.
