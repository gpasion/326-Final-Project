Title: Banana Apple Carrot Bake

Cook Time: 75 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Servings: 1

Kitchen Utensils: 
- Baking dish
- Vegetable peeler
- Blender
- Mixing spoon
- Cutting board
- Knife

Ingredients:
- 6 OZ of apple
- 4 OZ of banana
- 5 OZ of carrot

Instructions: 

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.

2. Peel and chop the apple and carrots into cubes. Also, chop the banana.

3. In a blender, put the chopped fruits and carrot. Blend until you get a fine paste. 

4. Transfer the promise to a baking dish and flatten it with a mixing spoon.

5. Put the baking dish in the preheated oven and bake for 75 minutes.

6. Once it's done, remove it from the oven and let it cool down.

7. Your Banana Apple Carrot Bake is ready to be served. Enjoy this healthy and tasty treat. 

Note: The cooking time may vary depending on the oven. Please check the bake after 45 minutes. If it's still soft, continue baking for another 30 minutes or until it's done. Please supervise the oven while baking to avoid over-cooking or burning.
