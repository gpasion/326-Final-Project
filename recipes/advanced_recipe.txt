Title: Baked Caramelized Banana-Apple-Carrot Delight 

Cook Time: 95 minutes 

Difficulty: Intermediate 

Servings: 1 

Required Kitchen Utensils: 
- Oven
- Knife 
- Baking Tray 
- Measuring Cups
- Peeler

Ingredients:
- 6 OZ of banana 
- 6 OZ of apples 
- 3 OZ of carrots
- 1 OZ of sugar (from apple and banana)

Instructions:

1. Start by preheating the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit or 190 degrees Celsius. 

2. While the oven is preheating, peel the banana, apple, and carrot. Chop them into small cubes.

3. Arrange the chopped fruits and carrots onto a baking tray evenly.

4. Put the tray into the oven and set a timer for 45 minutes. 

5. After 45 minutes, remove the tray from the oven and sprinkle the fruits and carrot pieces with the natural sugar from the apple and banana.

6. Put the tray back into the oven and decrease the temperature to 350 degrees Fahrenheit or 175 degrees Celsius. Bake for an additional 50 minutes or until the fruits and carrot pieces are well caramelized. 

7. After the baking is finished, remove the tray from the oven and let it cool for 10-15 minutes.

8. Serve this delectable dish as a warm dessert or enjoy it as a healthy snack.

Note: This recipe is highly customizable. Feel free to adjust the quantity of each ingredient based on your preference. Also, ensure to keep an eye on your dessert to prevent it from burning.
