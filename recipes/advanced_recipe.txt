Title: Fusion Delicacy – Salmon Sashimi with Apple, Banana, and Carrot Purée 

Cook Time: 90+ minutes 
Difficulty: Advanced

Kitchen Utensils:
- Chef's knife
- Cutting board
- Vegetable peeler
- Blender or food processor
- Fine mesh strainer
- Large bowl
- Whisk
- Serving platter

Ingredients (in oz):
- 8 oz fresh salmon sashimi
- 1 apple, peeled, cored, and roughly chopped
- 1 banana, peeled and roughly chopped
- 1 carrot, peeled and roughly chopped
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Salmon:
   - Using a sharp chef's knife, slice the fresh salmon into thin sashimi slices. Arrange on a serving platter and keep refrigerated until serving.

2. Create the Apple, Banana, and Carrot Purée:
   - In a blender or food processor, combine the chopped apple, banana, and carrot.
   - Blend until smooth, adding a splash of milk if needed to achieve a creamy consistency.
   - Pass the purée through a fine mesh strainer into a large bowl. Discard any solids left in the strainer.
   - Whisk in the sugar and milk until well combined. Set aside.

3. Marinate the Salmon:
   - Gently pour half of the apple, banana, and carrot purée over the prepared salmon sashimi slices.
   - Ensure all the slices are coated evenly. Cover the platter with plastic wrap and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour or up to overnight.

4. Plate and Serve:
   - Before serving, drain the excess marinade from the salmon sashimi carefully.
   - Arrange the marinated salmon slices on a clean serving platter.
   - Optional: Garnish with thin apple or carrot slices for an elegant presentation.
   - Serve the Fusion Delicacy - Salmon Sashimi with Apple, Banana, and Carrot Purée chilled and enjoy!

Note: This advanced recipe requires precise knife skills and food handling techniques. Ensure you are confident in handling raw fish before attempting.
