Title: Gourmet Apple-Banana Carrot Delight

Cook Time: 90 minutes

Difficulty: Advanced

Servings: 1

Kitchen Utensils: 
- Knife
- Blender
- Grater
- Saucepan
- Oven
- Mixing Bowl
- Baking Tray

Ingredients: 
- 3 OZ of Apple
- 2 OZ of Banana
- 4 OZ of Carrot

Instructions: 

1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  
2. Finely grate the apple, banana, and the carrot. 

3. Combine them in a mixing bowl and allow them to sit for about 10 minutes. This allows for the natural sugars in the fruits to start melding together, creating a base for your dish.

4. Transfer the fruit mixture to a saucepan and cook on medium heat for approximately 30 minutes. Watch for the mixture to start bubbling and for the fruits and carrot to soften.

5. After cooking, set it aside to cool for a while. Once it cools, you should have a chunky, fragrant fruit and carrot mixture.

6. Once it has cooled down, put the mixture into a blender. Blend on low speed until you have a smooth mixture.

7. Pour the blended mixture into a baking tray.

8. Place the baking tray into the preheated oven and bake for 45 minutes. This will slowly caramelize the sugars in the fruits, giving your dish a complex, gourmet flavor that takes time and patience to achieve.

9. After 45 minutes, remove the tray from the oven and allow it to cool.

10. The final result should be a deeply colored, aromatic dessert-like dish that perfectly showcases the individual tastes of the apple, banana, and carrot while also combining them into something truly unique and gourmet.

This advanced recipe is not only a testament to the unique blend of flavors that can be created with apple, banana, and carrot, but it's also a showcase of patience, delicacy, and gourmet cooking techniques. Enjoy this Apple-Banana Carrot Delight - a testament to the gourmand in you.
