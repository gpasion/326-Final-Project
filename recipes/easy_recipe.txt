Title: ABC Smoothie (Apple, Banana, Carrot)

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Servings: 1 

Kitchen Utensils:
- Blender
- Peeler
- Knife
- Measuring cups

Ingredients: 
- 5 OZ of Apple
- 5 OZ of Banana
- 5 OZ of Carrot

Instructions:
1. Start with washing the apple and carrot thoroughly under running water.
2. Peel the carrot and apple using the peeler. Then cut the carrot, apple, and banana into small pieces.
3. Place the pieces into the blender.
4. Blend on high speed until all ingredients are completely combined and the mixture is smooth. This should take about 5 minutes.
5. Pour the smoothie into a glass.
6. Your ABC Smoothie is ready to serve. Enjoy your healthy and fresh homemade smoothie!
