Title: ABC (Apple, Banana, Carrot) Baked Delight

Cook time: 35 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Servings: 1

Kitchen Utensils:
- Oven
- Baking Tray
- Knife
- Peeler
- Blender
- Mixing Bowl
- Measuring Scale

Ingredients:
- 4 OZ of Apple
- 2 OZ of Banana
- 4 OZ of Carrot

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.

2. Peel the apple and carrot, then dice them into small pieces.

3. Place the diced apple, carrot and banana into your blender. Blend until you get a smooth mixture.

4. Pour the mixture into your mixing bowl.

5. Place your baking tray in the oven and bake the mixture for 20 minutes.

6. After 20 minutes, take the tray out of the oven and let it cool down for a few minutes.

7. Your ABC (Apple, Banana, Carrot) Baked Delight is ready to serve. Enjoy!
