Title: Sweet and Savory Salmon Sashimi Bowl

Cook Time: 30 minutes
Difficulty: Easy

Kitchen Utensils:
- Knife
- Cutting board
- Mixing bowl
- Whisk

Ingredients:
- 4 oz Salmon Sashimi
- 1 Apple, thinly sliced
- 1 Banana, sliced
- 1 Carrot, julienned
- 2 tbsp Sugar
- 1/4 cup Milk
- 6 oz Cooked Chicken Breast, shredded

Step-by-step Instructions:

1. Start by preparing the sauce. In a mixing bowl, combine the sugar and milk. Whisk until the sugar has dissolved completely.

2. Add the shredded chicken breast to the bowl and mix well, ensuring all the chicken is coated in the sauce. Set aside for flavors to meld.

3. Arrange the salmon sashimi, sliced apple, sliced banana, and julienned carrot on a serving plate or in individual bowls.

4. Pour the chicken breast mixture over the salmon sashimi bowl, distributing evenly.

5. Serve immediately and enjoy the sweet and savory flavors of this delicious salmon sashimi bowl!

Note: If desired, you can garnish the bowl with sesame seeds, chopped cilantro, or sliced green onions for added flavor and presentation. This recipe is versatile, and you can adjust the quantities of ingredients to your preference. Enjoy!
