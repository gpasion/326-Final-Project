Title: Chicken Breast with Sour Cream & Veggie Stew

Cook Time: 45 Minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Kitchen Utensils: 
- Skillet 
- Spatula 
- Knife 
- Mixing Bowl 
- Measuring cups

Ingredient List: 
- 16 OZ of Chicken Breast
- 6 OZ of Tomato
- 7 OZ of Potato
- 5 OZ of Sour Cream
- 2 OZ of Butter
- 4 OZ of Bell Pepper

Instructions:

1. First, wash and chop the chicken breast into cubes. Also, peel the potatoes and cut them into small pieces. Chop the tomatoes and bell peppers as well. 

2. Next, heat up your skillet on a medium flame and melt the butter. 

3. Now add the chicken cubes into the skillet. Cook until the chicken turns white and firm, usually takes about 5-7 minutes.

4. Add the chopped tomatoes, bell peppers and potatoes into the skillet. Stir well and cover it. Let it cook for around 10-15 minutes, or until the veggies are cooked and soft.

5. Lastly, lower the heat and add the sour cream into the skillet. Stir well and let it cook for 2-3 more minutes. 

6. Your Chicken Breast with Sour Cream & Veggie Stew is ready! Serve hot. 

Enjoy your meal!
