Title: Creamy Tomato Chicken

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Kitchen Utensils:
- Cutting board
- Knife
- Medium-sized pot with lid
- Skillet
- Wooden spoon
- Measuring cups and spoons

Ingredients:
- 8 oz chicken breast, boneless and skinless
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 medium-sized onion, diced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 14 oz crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup sour cream
- Salt and pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions:
1. Start by cutting the chicken breast into bite-sized cubes.
2. In a medium-sized pot, melt the butter over medium heat.
3. Add the diced onion and bell pepper to the pot and sauté until they become tender and slightly golden.
4. Add the chicken breast cubes to the pot and cook until they are browned on all sides.
5. Meanwhile, peel and cube the potatoes.
6. Once the chicken is browned, add the cubed potatoes to the pot.
7. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and stir everything together.
8. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot with a lid, and let the mixture simmer for about 20 minutes or until the potatoes are cooked through.
9. Stir in the sour cream, season with salt and pepper to taste, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to warm through.
10. Serve the creamy tomato chicken over rice, pasta, or with your choice of side dish.

Enjoy your delicious Creamy Tomato Chicken with the perfect blend of flavors from tomatoes, potatoes, and sour cream!
